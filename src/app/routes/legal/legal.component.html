ASN.1

ASN.1 (Abstract Syntax Notation version 1) is a language designed for defining messages inside protocols. It is officially specified by the ITU organization. See the Wikipedia article for an introduction.

You can also find the official specifications:

  • X.680 - ASN.1 specification
  • X.690 - BER/CER/DER specification
